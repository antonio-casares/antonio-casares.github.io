Antonio Casares Santos

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Theoretical Computer Science at the University of Warsaw (Poland), working in the group of Mikołaj Bojańczyk.

My research interests lie in the intersection of logic, formal languages and games on graphs. More speifically, my research focuses on ω-automata, transducers and algorithms for solving infinite duration games.

I received my doctoral degree in 2023 from the University of Bordeaux, under the supervision of Thomas Colcombet, Nathanaël Fijalkow and Igor Walukiewicz. Before that, I obtained my master's degree in Mathematical Logic and Foundations of Computer Science at Université de Paris-Cité (France).

You will find more information in my CV.
