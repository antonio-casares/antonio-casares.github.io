Antonio Casares Santos

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Theoretical Computer Science at the RPTU, University of Kaiserslautern-Landau (Germany), working in the group of Anthony Lin.

My research interests lie in the intersection of logic, formal languages and games on graphs. More specifically, my research focuses on ω-automata, algorithms for solving infinite duration games and finite model theory.

Before coming to Kaiserslautern, I was a postdoc at the University of Warsaw (Poland) with Mikołaj Bojańczyk. I received my doctoral degree in 2023 from the University of Bordeaux, under the supervision of Nathanaël Fijalkow and Igor Walukiewicz. Before that, I obtained my master's degree in Mathematical Logic and Foundations of Computer Science at Université de Paris-Cité (France).
